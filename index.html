About Us

Munnar Dormitory offers budget-friendly accommodation for backpackers, students, and group travelers. With cozy bunk beds, shared spaces, and essential amenities, we provide a clean and safe stay amidst the stunning beauty of Munnar’s tea plantations and hills.
